Redux isn't just "another way to do state". It's a professional architectural pattern for large-scale applications.

When to use Redux?

  • Many global states: Not just Auth, but complex domain data.
  • Many teams: Working on different parts of the state tree.
  • Need DevTools: Time-travel debugging and state snapshotting.
  • Need Middleware: For logging, crash reporting, or complex API handling.
  • Large Scale App: Where Prop Drilling and Context nesting become a nightmare.
