➕ Operators in JS

Operators perform some operation on single or multiple operands (data values) and produce a result.

1. Arithmetic Operators

Operator Meaning Example
+ Addition 10 + 5 = 15
- Subtraction 10 - 5 = 5
* Multiplication 10 * 2 = 20
/ Division 10 / 2 = 5
% Modulus (Remainder) 10 % 3 = 1

2. Comparison Operators

Operator Meaning
== Equal to
=== Strict Equal (checks Value + Type)
!= Not Equal to
> / < />= / <=< /td> Greater/Less than

3. Logical Operators

  • && (AND): True if BOTH are true.
  • || (OR): True if ANY ONE is true.
  • ! (NOT): Reverses the boolean value.
