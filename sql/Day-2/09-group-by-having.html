2. HAVING - Filter After Grouping
üëâ Important Difference
‚ö†Ô∏è WHERE vs HAVING:
  • WHERE filters rows BEFORE grouping
  • HAVING filters groups AFTER grouping
üß† Example Code 
-- Cities with more than 2 students
SELECT city, COUNT(*) 
FROM students 
GROUP BY city 
HAVING COUNT(*) > 2;
üìù Result: Shows only cities that have more than 2 students
üìä WHERE vs HAVING Comparison 
-- WHERE: Filter before grouping
SELECT city, COUNT(*) 
FROM students 
WHERE age > 20          -- Filter individual rows first
GROUP BY city;

-- HAVING: Filter after grouping
SELECT city, COUNT(*) 
FROM students 
GROUP BY city 
HAVING COUNT(*) > 2;    -- Filter groups after counting
‚úÖ Complete Example (WHERE + GROUP BY + HAVING) 
-- Step by step:
-- 1. WHERE: Get only active students
-- 2. GROUP BY: Group by city
-- 3. HAVING: Show cities with more than 1 active student
SELECT city, COUNT(*) 
FROM students 
WHERE status = 'active'    -- Filter rows first
GROUP BY city              -- Group by city
HAVING COUNT(*) > 1;       -- Filter groups after
‚úÖ More HAVING Examples
